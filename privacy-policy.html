Privacy Policy for My Reminders

Last updated: February 7, 2026

1. Introduction

My Reminders ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Reminders: Titles, notes, dates, times, and categories you create
  • Voice Input: Voice recordings are processed on-device for voice-to-text conversion and are not stored or transmitted
  • Google Account Information: Email address and basic profile information when you sign in with Google

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Device Information: For scheduling notifications and alarms
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app to improve functionality

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide reminder notifications at scheduled times
  • Sync your reminders across your devices using Firebase
  • Enable voice input functionality for creating reminders
  • Improve app performance and user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Local Storage: Reminders are stored on your device
  • Cloud Sync: Data is encrypted and stored in Google Firebase (only when signed in)
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication and cloud synchronization
  • Google Play Services: For app functionality

These services have their own privacy policies. We do not share your data with any other third parties.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data at any time through the app
  • Delete your reminders individually or all at once
  • Sign out to stop cloud synchronization
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: redboxstudios.app@gmail.com

10. Permissions

Our app requests the following permissions:

  • RECORD_AUDIO: To enable voice input for creating reminders (processed on-device only)
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM: To deliver reminders at precise times
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To show reminder notifications
  • INTERNET: To sync reminders with Firebase when signed in
